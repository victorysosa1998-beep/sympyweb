Plain English: Sympy stores your name, language preference, and conversation history so the AI remembers you. We use OpenAI for responses and Firebase for authentication. We never sell your data.
01

Information We Collect

  • Account info: Your name and email via Firebase Authentication.
  • Conversation data: Messages, images you share, and AI responses.
  • Voice data: Speech processed in real time via LiveKit and OpenAI STT. Raw audio is never stored.
  • Usage data: Call durations, session timestamps, daily quota usage.
  • Device identifier: A stable ID tied to your Firebase account, used for daily call limits.
  • Mood & streak data: Emotional tone and daily usage streaks for personalisation.
02

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve the Sympy service
  • Help the AI remember your name, language, and conversation context
  • Enforce the 5-minute daily voice call limit
  • Generate conversation summaries shown in the app
  • Track mood and usage streaks for personalisation

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

03

Data Storage & Security

Conversation history is stored in Redis with a 30-day retention period. After 30 days of inactivity, your session data is automatically deleted.

Security measures include HTTPS/WSS encryption, API key authentication, Firebase Authentication, and no storage of raw audio recordings.

04

AI Conversations & Memory

Sympy is powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o models. Messages are transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details.

Your conversation history helps the AI remember your preferences and generate summaries. Images sent during chat are processed by GPT-4o vision and are not stored after processing.

Children's Privacy

Sympy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has used the service, contact us and we will delete their data immediately.

07

Your Rights

  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Correction — Update your account information
  • Portability — Request your conversation history

Email us to exercise any of these rights. We respond within 30 days.
